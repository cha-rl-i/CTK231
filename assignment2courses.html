200 Level Classes

201 - Basic Skills In The Digital Arts (3)
Introduction to the history, theories, current trends, practices, and core media in the digital arts.

202 - Foundations: Audio Theory (3)
Fundamentals of audio theory as applied to the physics and aesthetics of sound.

203 - Foundations: Recording and Sampling (3)
Technical skills required for recording and sampling of instruments and voice.

204 - Foundations: Music Concepts (3)
Foundational music concepts, including basic theory, history, genres, and forms.

208 - Audio Production I (3)
Audio production techniques within a digital audio workstation (DAW).

231 - Web Design and Development (3)
Theory, technical skills, and design concepts for creating standards-compliant websites. Formerly CTK 301.

232 - Computer Programming for Creatives (3)
Object-oriented programming concepts for interactive design, UI, data visualization, and gaming. Formerly CTK 302.

240 - Sound Design 1: Beginning Digital Audio (3)
Introduction to digital audio and aesthetic principles of sound.

260 - Arts Technology Performance Studio (3)
Creative and interdisciplinary experiences integrating computer technology into performance.

300 Level Classes

303 - Advanced Web Design and Development (3)
Advanced programming for dynamic websites and interactive applications.

320 - Programming Dynamic Media (3)
Dataflow programming for media performance, installations, and generative art.

330 - Web Video (3)
Technical skills and concepts for web-based fictional and documentary video.

340 - Sound Design 2: Advanced Digital Audio (3)
Advanced digital audio and aesthetic principles of sound.

345 - Sequencing And Digital Audio (3)
Music synthesis through MIDI, sound editing/recording, and integration with digital audio.

351 - Game Design I (3)
Foundational game design class on engagement, fun, and motivation.

354 - User Experience Design for Games (3)
Explores interface design in gaming and its impact on user experience.

377 - Motion Graphics (3)
Create motion graphics with compositing, animation, and VFX for broadcast, web, VR, and AR.
